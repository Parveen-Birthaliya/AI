- Free tier for virality
- Paid tiers for power users
- Enterprise custom pricing

**Best for:** Developer tools, productivity apps

### Usage-Based
- Pay per API call/use
- No monthly fees
- Scales with customer success

**Best for:** APIs, infrastructure tools

### Subscription
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Predictable cash flow
- Easy to forecast

**Best for:** SaaS products

### Transaction Fee
- Commission on marketplace
- No fees unless customer succeeds
- Aligned incentives

**Best for:** Marketplaces, platforms

## üí° KEY INSIGHTS FOR TECH FOUNDERS

**1. Build for 100 customers, not 1 million:**
- Deep customer relationships
- High ARPU = sustainable business
- Enterprise deals can be ‚Çπ10L-1Cr each

**2. AI is your unfair advantage:**
- Build in weeks what took months
- Compete with teams of 100
- Offer "magic" user experiences

**3. Distribution > Product (sometimes):**
- Great product with no users = failure
- Good product with distribution = success
- Build distribution from day one

**4. Niche down initially:**
- Be #1 in small market
- Then expand to adjacent markets
- "Instagram for doctors" beats "Instagram"

**5. Solve your own problems:**
- You understand the pain deeply
- You know the target customer (you)
- Easier to validate and iterate
